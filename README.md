# web-dev-barangay
 Barangay Web Dev Community Portal - Final Project for NTC_PC9

My chosen scenario, Scenario B: The Community Resource Hub, reflects the NTC mission in two key ways.

First, building character. Building this barangay portal taught me to think beyond code. I had to understand the needs of residents, barangay officials, and community members — people who rely on accessible information and efficient services. This developed my sense of empathy and responsibility. I learned that being a developer is not just about technical skill, but about using that skill to serve others with integrity.

Second, being responsive to national needs. Barangays are the foundation of Philippine governance. Yet many still depend on manual processes like paper forms and physical bulletin boards. By creating a digital platform with announcement feeds, online certificate requests with JavaScript validation, and an issue reporting system, this project directly addresses a real gap — making local government services faster, more accessible, and available to residents anytime, even without visiting the barangay hall.

In summary, this project shows that even as students, we can use HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and JavaScript to build tools that uplift communities — reflecting NTC's mission of forming competent professionals who are also citizens of character, ready to serve the Philippines.
